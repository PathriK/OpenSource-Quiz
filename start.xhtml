Welcome

JSS Academy of Technical Education, Bangalore Open Source University Meetup (OSUM) (pronounced "awesome") with support from Sun Microsystems, Inc. presents the open-source quiz.

This quiz is designed to test your understanding of some of the open-source technologies. Take this quiz to enrich your knowledge about the world of open-source!

What's more, there're loads of goodies to be won including:

  • First Prize: a limited-edition "Innovate on OpenSolaris" travel mug
  • Subsequent prizes:
    • Sun Microsystems, Inc. pens and key-chains
    • a few limited-edition OpenSolaris wrist-bands and OSUM.SUN.COM - CHANGE (Y)OUR WORLD wrist-bands
    • NetBeans 6.5 DVDs

This quiz is open to all the students, who are currently studying in Information Science & Engineering branch of JSS Academy of Technical Education, Bangalore, only.

As this is our first time, we're purposely conducting this quiz on a small scale. Once this quiz goes on well, we'll surely conduct another one for the whole college. Other branches, sorry, but keep an eye out for the next quiz!

So, what're you waiting for? Don't miss this opportunity to be a part of the greatest revolution the world has ever seen - the open-source revolution!

Let's get started

So, all set to get started? Alright then, all you have to do is...

  1. Grab your friend and form a team of 2 (two) members.
  2. Go to Open-Source University Meetup website and register yourselves! It's a website for student developers worldwide (like you!) You can share, learn, collaraborate and innovate.
  3. Next, join our JSSATE OSUM community and post a reply to the open-source quiz thread proving your registration.

    Registration on the OSUM website and our college community is mandatory to qualify as a winner.

  4. Register your details with your class rep and obtain the activation code.
  5. Use the form below to register your team once again online and take the quiz.

Read these rules carefully before taking the quiz...

  1. The quiz will start once you press the Submit button below.

  2. You have exactly 15 minutes to complete the quiz. Your time will start immediately when you see the question paper. When the time is up, your answers will be automatically submitted (provided JavaScript is enabled) or else you must see to it that you submit the answers before the time is up.

  3. Be sure you're using a JavaScript-enabled browser and you've enabled JavaScript.

  4. We will show a count-down timer on your screen, but do not rely on it, use your own timer/stop-watches for keeping time. Most mobile phones are equipped with a timer/stop-watch; use it.

  5. Right-click and Copy the activation code above, you'll need to enter it again on the next page.

  6. If you submit the wrong activation code on the next page, your activation code will be deactivated.

  7. If you submit the answers after the alotted time duration, you'll be disqualified.

  8. If the above information is found to be inconsistent or wrong, you'll be disqualified.

  9. The decision of the organizers is final. No further communication will be entertained.

  10. Do not use your browser's Back button (unless instructed to do so by us) or mess around with the application; your activation code will be deactivated.

  11. In case of a tie, the team that submitted the answers in the smallest amount of time, will be considered as better.

  12. Clicking on the Submit button will be deemed as your acceptance of our rules.
